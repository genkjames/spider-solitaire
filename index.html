Rules

  1. You have 8 minutes to get 5 sequences of cards that have a set of K to A.

  2. Click on the card you want to move first, and then click on a card that has one less value than the previous card.

  3. You can move more than one card to another slot, as long as the cards are lined up in order from the greatest value to the least value.

    i.e, K -> Q -> J

  4. If you run out of moves you can click on the deck in the upper left corner to distribute more cards to the playing field.

  5. The Undo button is your best friend in this game. It can only be used to undo moves from the last sequence of cards from K to A you were able to get in order.
